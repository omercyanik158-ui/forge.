## Subtitle

## Short description

FORGE helps you build workout plans, track meals, monitor water intake, and follow your weekly progress in one focused fitness app.

## Long description

FORGE brings your training, nutrition, hydration, and progress tracking into one clean flow.

With FORGE you can:

- create your own workout plans
- browse and filter exercises
- log meals and daily calories
- track water intake
- follow workout and strength progress
- unlock premium plans and advanced insights

Designed for everyday consistency, FORGE helps you stay close to your goal without turning fitness tracking into a chore.
