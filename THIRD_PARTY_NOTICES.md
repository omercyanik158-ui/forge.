and package metadata are available in the installed package directories and
the npm registry.

## Exercise data

The exercise catalog is generated from
[`yuhonas/free-exercise-db`](https://github.com/yuhonas/free-exercise-db), which
is published under the Unlicense. Exercise images are loaded from the upstream
repository at runtime and remain subject to the upstream project's licensing
and availability.
